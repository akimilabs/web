About Our Team

At Akimilabs, we're a dynamic team of innovators and problem-solvers passionate about revolutionizing productivity through AI. Our diverse backgrounds in software architecture, engineering, and product development drive us to create solutions that empower individuals and collaborative teams to achieve more.

Founded by David and Anissa Agahchen, our company combines technical excellence with a deep understanding of user needs. We believe in building technology that thinks with you, not for you, creating tools that enhance human potential rather than replace it.

  • Partners interested in co-developing frameworks for evaluating the Think → Do → Reflect cycle's impact on well-being and performance

If you share our vision of Attached Intelligence as a collaborative force—where AI works with people, not for or against them—we'd love to collaborate. Join us in empowering Modern Achievers to achieve mindful productivity and personal growth through balanced intellect, action, and emotion.
